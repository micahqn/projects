Wilhelm Wundt - German Professor "strict and middle-aged," first person to create a psychology experiment (press button to hear sound, and another when you process you heard it)

G. Stanley Hall - Created the first psychology laboratory at John Hopkins university. Student of Wundt

Edward Bradford Titchener - Created structuralism, student of Wundt. 

William James - Created functionalism, philosopher psychologist, joyous man, wrote the Principles of Psychology

Mary Whiton Calkins - Woman mentored by William James at Harvard and became the president of the APA, was denied a PHD

Margaret Floy Washburn - Woman, first PHD, wrote the Animal Mind, became 2nd female APA president, wrote a thesis about Wundt. 

Sigmund Freud - The founder of psychoanalytic psychology
