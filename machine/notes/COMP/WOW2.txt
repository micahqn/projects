Circumspect - Careful, cautious, before making a decision

Clandestine - Kept secret, because it is forbidden or disliked

Coalesce - To come together to become a mass or whole

Coerce - To persuade via threats and violence

Compendium - A precise collection of information, concise and niche

Conflagration - A great fire that destroys lots of land or property

Consensus - General agreement among a group

Conspicuous - Standing out and obvious, used negatively

Contrite - Deep remorse, sincere regret

Convivial - Friendly in a bubbly, loud, way
